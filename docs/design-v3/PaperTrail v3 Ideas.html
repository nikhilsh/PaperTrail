Shelf widgets

Home-screen + lock-screen widgets in the paper aesthetic: coverage ring, next window closing. The library sits on the shelf you look at 100×/day — free daily retention.

Quick win

Five ways to shelve

Scan receipt · photo of the thing · forward an email · barcode · say it aloud. Kills the #1 funnel leak (adding feels like homework). Email forwarding = items appear on their own.

Adoption
IMPACT ●●●  EFFORT ●●○From Centriq · nobody does it well
Recall watch

Match model numbers against safety-recall feeds. "We watch the recall lists so you don't have to." Even a quiet ✓ NO RECALLS row builds enormous trust — and it's a push notification users WANT.

Plus
IMPACT ●●○  EFFORT ●○○Warranty Keeper's top complaint
Multiple coverages per item

TV panel 3 yrs, parts 1 yr; car battery vs bodywork. One item, several coverage lines with separate clocks. Rivals literally can't — cheap differentiator, fits the passport design as extra rows.

Quick win
IMPACT ●●○  EFFORT ●●○Gap · after-purchase life
The service ledger

Repairs, part swaps, servicing logged per item — a maintenance history in the timeline spine. Feeds the claim packet ("serviced on schedule") and the resale packet. Sticky data moat.

Depth
IMPACT ●●○  EFFORT ●●○Original — nobody has it
Pass it on (resale packet)

Selling the TV? Export a buyer-facing packet: proof of purchase, service history, remaining transferable warranty. Listings with proof sell higher — and every packet is a PaperTrail ad in a stranger's hands.

Plus · viral
IMPACT ●●○  EFFORT ●○○From Centriq
Manual on file

Attach or auto-fetch the PDF manual per item. "Where's the manual" is the same drawer-panic as "where's the receipt" — one more reason the library is the only place to look.

Depth
IMPACT ●●○  EFFORT ●○○Platform-native · rivals ignore
Siri & Spotlight

App Intents: "Hey Siri, is the washer still under warranty?" → passport card. Receipts findable from iOS search. Apple-native depth HomeProof and Under My Roof haven't touched.

Quick win
IMPACT ●○○  EFFORT ●○○Parking lot — resist for now
Not doing

AI chat assistant (novelty, breaks butler voice) · vehicle/pet/medical records (scope sprawl — the Under My Roof trap) · social/leaderboards · ads or data monetization (kills the entire privacy position).
