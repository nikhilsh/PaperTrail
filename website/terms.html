PaperTrail Plus subscriptions

PaperTrail's core features are free. PaperTrail Plus is an optional paid membership that unlocks extras such as household sharing, insurance reports, hand-off packets, repair timelines, and manuals on file. Plus is offered as:

Billing, renewal & cancellation

  • Payment is charged to your Apple Account at confirmation of purchase.
  • Subscriptions renew automatically unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your account is charged for renewal within 24 hours before the current period ends.

Your data & responsibilities

Your records live on your device and in your own private iCloud account — PaperTrail has no servers holding your data, so you are responsible for keeping iCloud sync enabled or exporting backups if you want your records preserved across devices or after device loss.
