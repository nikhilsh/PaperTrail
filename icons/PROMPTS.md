# PaperTrail Icon Prompt Pack

Black-themed icon concepts for PaperTrail.

## Recommended generation order

1. **Receipt Minimal** — safest and clearest
2. **PaperTrail Monogram** — best brand potential
3. **Archive Stack** — strongest "record vault" feeling

## Shared guidance

- Design for **iOS app icon readability first**
- Prefer **one central symbol**
- No text inside the icon
- Avoid tiny receipt text/details
- Keep backgrounds dark: black / obsidian / charcoal
- Accent colors should be restrained

## Shared negative prompt

`text, letters, words, watermark, busy background, realistic photo, multiple objects, tiny details, low contrast, cartoon mascot, skeuomorphic clutter, gradient overload, glossy plastic, crypto logo style, generic finance icon, app store screenshot, mockup phone frame`

## Shared style suffix

`iOS app icon, centered composition, minimal premium branding, strong silhouette, no tiny details, no text, no watermark, black luxury theme, subtle depth, clean edges, high contrast, suitable for Apple app icon, simple enough to read at small sizes`

## Variant palette suggestions

### Variant A — Warm premium
- background: matte black
- primary: ivory / warm white
- accent: muted gold

### Variant B — Crisp modern
- background: black
- primary: white
- accent: electric blue

### Variant C — Soft serious
- background: charcoal
- primary: silver
- accent: dark emerald

## Suggested workflow

Generate 3-4 variations for each of the top 3 concepts first:
- receipt-minimal
- monogram-p
- archive-stack

Then shortlist 2 and refine only those.
